Detecting Intrusions in Industrial Internet of Things

Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) will replace many wired/wireless industrial components and will be required to be highly reliable and secure. Ensuring confidentiality, integrity, and availability against intrusion threats is essential. Intrusion detection systems are one of the key components in securing IIoT.
